Chris O'Driscoll


Chris O’Driscoll is a Hudson Valley singer-songwriter whose music blends thoughtful lyrics with expressive, understated guitar work. His songs carry a quiet emotional weight, shaped by the landscapes and small moments that inspire him. With a warm, unpolished voice and a grounded presence on stage, Chris creates performances that feel intimate and honest, whether he’s in a crowded room or a quiet corner venue. His recordings capture the same simplicity and sincerity that define his live shows, offering listeners a clear sense of place, feeling, and craft.
